About Vibe Guard

Version

1.0.0

What is Vibe Guard?

Vibe Guard is a browser extension that uses Gemini 3's Advanced Visual Reasoning capabilities to detect deepfakes and AI-generated content in real-time.

How it Works

  1. Analyzes images and videos on social media feeds
  2. Uses Gemini AI to identify visual artifacts and inconsistencies
  3. Cross-references claims with trusted fact-checking databases
  4. Provides a credibility score from 0-100%

Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. We do not store or share your media analysis results beyond what's necessary for analysis. All processing is done securely.
